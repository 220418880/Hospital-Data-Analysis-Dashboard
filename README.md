# Hospital-Data-Analysis

Interactive hospital analytics dashboard providing insights into patient care, procedure utilization, healthcare costs, and organizational performance.

Objective of the Dashboard:
To provide a comprehensive view of hospital operations and patient activity by monitoring key healthcare metrics such as patient encounters, length of stay, procedure utilization, admission trends, and treatment costs. The dashboard enables healthcare stakeholders to identify operational patterns, evaluate resource utilization, and support data-driven decision-making.

Healthcare Problem Being Analyzed:
Hospitals need actionable insights into patient utilization, encounter classifications, procedure volumes, and healthcare costs. Without a consolidated analytical view, identifying trends, inefficiencies, and resource demands becomes difficult. This dashboard helps uncover patterns in patient activity and healthcare service delivery to support informed decision-making.

Key Insights Covered by This Dashboard:
Total Patients and Encounters
Average Length of Stay (LOS)
Average Cost per Visit
Admission Trends by Hour
Encounter Class Distribution
Procedure Coverage Analysis
Gender Distribution of Patients
Cost Analysis by Encounter Class
Cost Trends Over Time
